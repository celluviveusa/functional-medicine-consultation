Functional vs. Conventional Medicine

Both traditional and functional medicine play important roles, but their goals differ.

Conventional medicine focuses on acute care — treating specific symptoms, infections, or emergencies. It’s reactive by nature.

Functional medicine is proactive. It looks for the underlying causes of imbalance — whether that’s inflammation, nutritional deficiency, toxin buildup, or hormonal misalignment.

At CelluVive, we use functional insights to restore communication between your systems — helping your body heal itself the way nature intended.

This approach doesn’t replace modern medicine. It complements it by addressing the why instead of only the what.
