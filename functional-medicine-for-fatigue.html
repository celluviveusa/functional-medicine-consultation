Functional Medicine vs. Conventional Medicine

The two systems share the goal of better health, but their approaches differ fundamentally. Conventional medicine treats disease after it appears, focusing on symptoms and immediate relief. It’s essential in emergencies but limited for chronic, recurring issues.

Functional medicine, in contrast, seeks to understand the mechanisms that lead to imbalance before disease develops. It identifies how genetics, environment, and lifestyle intersect — and addresses dysfunction at the source.

At CelluVive, we believe both models have value, but Functional Medicine goes a step further: it restores the body’s natural communication and teaches you how to maintain it. This proactive model doesn’t just keep you alive; it helps you live better.
