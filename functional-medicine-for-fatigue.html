The Science of Functional Medicine

Traditional medicine treats disease after it appears. Functional medicine focuses on why it appeared in the first place.

It’s an investigative model that looks for underlying causes rather than symptoms. Instead of dividing the body into specialties, it views it as one connected system — where hormones, nutrients, and neural signals interact continuously.

  • Gut inflammation can affect mood and immunity.
  • Sleep deprivation alters insulin sensitivity and hormone levels.
  • Chronic stress can deplete nutrients, leading to fatigue or brain fog.

By exploring these interactions, we identify the root triggers of imbalance — from nutrient deficiencies to environmental toxins — and address them through personalized interventions. At CelluVive, this process is guided by data, refined by experience, and sustained by partnership.
