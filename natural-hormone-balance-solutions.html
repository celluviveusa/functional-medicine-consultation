Inside the Functional Medicine Process

A Functional Medicine Consultation at CelluVive is a step-by-step collaboration designed to discover, correct, and sustain wellness.

  • Step 1 — Discovery Session
    Your journey begins with an open discussion. We listen carefully to your goals, challenges, and health history. Every detail helps us build context — the missing piece that typical check-ups often ignore.
  • Step 2 — 360° Evaluation
    We evaluate your medical background, nutrition, movement, emotional wellbeing, and environment. Patterns start to emerge — patterns that reveal how your systems have been compensating for imbalance.
  • Step 3 — Advanced Testing
    Using targeted diagnostics, we examine hormones, metabolism, inflammation markers, and gut microbiome activity. These insights create a map of how your body is performing at the cellular level.
  • Step 4 — Personalized Blueprint
    Once data is gathered, we design a Functional Wellness Blueprint unique to you. It includes nutrition strategies, supplementation protocols, and lifestyle guidance focused on restoring function, not suppressing symptoms.
  • Step 5 — Implementation & Coaching
    A Functional Health Coach partners with you to put the plan into motion — providing education, accountability, and motivation to turn theory into practice.
  • Step 6 — Monitoring & Optimization
    We track your progress through follow-ups and retesting. As your biology improves, your plan evolves, ensuring that every step moves you closer to long-term balance.

Each consultation is dynamic — it adapts as you heal, allowing progress to be tangible and results to be sustainable.
