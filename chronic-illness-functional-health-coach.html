The Difference Between Conventional and Functional Medicine

Conventional medicine focuses on controlling symptoms through medication or procedures. Functional medicine focuses on uncovering why those symptoms appeared in the first place.

Traditional care is excellent for acute illness — infections, injuries, or emergencies. But for chronic or recurring problems like fatigue, inflammation, or hormone imbalance, it often stops short.

Functional Medicine looks deeper. It studies how your lifestyle, nutrition, environment, and stress influence your biology — then restores function by addressing those root causes.
