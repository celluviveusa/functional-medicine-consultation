Functional Medicine — How It Works

Functional medicine studies how and why illness develops and restores health by addressing its root causes. Instead of asking “What drug fixes this?” we ask “What system is out of balance — and why?”

We look at how different body systems communicate:

  • Hormonal networks that affect sleep, energy, and mood.
  • Gut health that influences immunity and brain function.
  • Nutritional pathways that control metabolism and repair.
  • Stress response systems that determine how your body adapts daily.

This approach doesn’t separate science from lifestyle; it combines both. By understanding how these systems work together, we can correct imbalances naturally and restore the body’s built-in intelligence.
