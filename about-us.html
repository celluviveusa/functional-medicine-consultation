About CelluVive — A New Era in Personalized Wellness

About CelluVive — A New Era in Personalized Wellness

At CelluVive, we believe that health isn’t just the absence of disease—it’s the presence of energy, clarity, and balance. Our approach is rooted in Functional Medicine, an evidence-based system that uncovers the root causes of imbalance rather than simply suppressing symptoms.

Founded in 2016, CelluVive emerged from a vision to create a space where modern diagnostics meet personalized care, and where healing is a journey of understanding—not guesswork. Today, we empower individuals to take charge of their biology, restore vitality, and thrive at every level—physical, mental, and cellular.

Our Origin Story — Built on Curiosity and Care

The founders of CelluVive—a collaborative team of physicians, functional medicine practitioners, clinical nutritionists, and wellness coaches—recognized a broken pattern in conventional care: people were receiving temporary relief, but not lasting solutions. It wasn’t that medicine was failing; it simply wasn’t asking the right questions.

Where others saw isolated symptoms, we saw a bigger picture. Where others treated numbers, we looked for patterns. We launched CelluVive to fill that gap—to become a clinic where investigation replaces assumption, and healing begins with a deeper understanding of the person behind the symptoms.

The name itself reflects this belief:

  • Cellu — representing the fundamental building block of life: the cell
  • Vive — a nod to vitality, vibrance, and living fully

Together, CelluVive means restoring life from the inside out.

Our Core Service: Functional Medicine Consultation

Our signature service—the Functional Medicine ConsultationFunctional Medicine Consultation—is a deep-dive into your health unlike any standard appointment. We don’t rush. We listen. We test. We investigate. And we build a roadmap designed for you.

Here’s what it includes:

1. Comprehensive Health History Review

We don’t just ask what’s wrong—we ask what happened. From childhood illnesses to daily routines, we map your unique timeline of imbalance.

2. Advanced Diagnostics

We utilize functional labs to assess key systems, including:

  • Hormonal balance
  • Gut microbiome and digestion
  • Blood sugar regulation
  • Detox pathways

  • Sleep/stress hormone cycles

3. Pattern Recognition & Root Cause Analysis

Symptoms are signals. We look at how your systems interact and identify the deeper cause behind your fatigue, brain fog, weight issues, skin problems, or recurring pain.

4. Personalized Protocol Creation

Based on your data, we design a practical plan that includes:

  • Food-as-medicine strategies
  • Targeted supplementation (not guesswork)
  • Stress and sleep optimization
  • Detox and gut restoration
  • Lifestyle adjustments that fit your life

5. Ongoing Coaching & Adjustments

Healing takes time, and we’re with you each step of the way. Our team ensures your plan evolves based on progress, retesting, and real-life challenges.
