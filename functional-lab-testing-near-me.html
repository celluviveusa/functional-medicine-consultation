Functional Medicine vs. Conventional Medicine

Conventional medicine focuses on short-term solutions — identifying a symptom and managing it through medication or intervention. It’s excellent for emergencies, but it often overlooks chronic patterns and systemic imbalance.

Functional Medicine, on the other hand, is proactive and preventative. It seeks to understand why symptoms appear and how systems interact to create them.

At CelluVive, this difference defines everything we do. We don’t label people with conditions — we study their unique biology to uncover what’s causing dysfunction. The goal is not just to remove pain but to restore full function — to bring your body back into alignment so it can heal and thrive naturally.
