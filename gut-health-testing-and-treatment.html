What Makes Functional Medicine Different

Conventional medicine asks: What disease does this person have? Functional Medicine asks: Why did this person develop these symptoms?

The difference lies in the focus. Traditional care targets the condition; Functional Medicine focuses on the root cause.

At CelluVive, we look at the body as one interconnected network. Hormones influence digestion, digestion affects immunity, and stress impacts every system in between. When one area falls out of alignment, others follow.

Functional Medicine isn’t alternative. It’s advanced — integrating the precision of modern science with a holistic understanding of how your body truly works.

Functional Medicine vs. Conventional Medicine

Conventional medicine specializes in treating acute illness — infections, injuries, emergencies. Functional Medicine focuses on the long game — preventing disease and reversing dysfunction before it becomes chronic.

Traditional care often isolates systems: heart, thyroid, gut, brain. Functional Medicine integrates them, revealing how one imbalance can ripple through the rest.

At CelluVive, we believe these models can work together — but we specialize in the deeper “why.” Our consultations identify what drives fatigue, inflammation, or hormonal shifts and address it directly through personalized strategies.
